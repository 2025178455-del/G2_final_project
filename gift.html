Instructions

  1. Cut open a toilet roll, flatten it, roll it into a cone, and glue it.
  2. Roll a thin strip of brown paper into a tight coil and glue the end.
  3. Cut a long strip of yellow paper and fringe one side to make petals.
  4. Fluff the fringe, glue the uncut edge, and wrap it around the brown coil to form a sunflower.
  5. Repeat to make several mini sunflowers.
  6. Place the flowers inside the cone and adjust until it looks neat and full.
  7. (Optional) Decorate with ribbon, stickers, glitter, or a tag.
